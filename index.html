AI in Action: Real Department Impacts

See how departments across disciplines are using Transform Learning to improve student outcomes and strengthen their programs.

Psychology Department

Outcome alignment analytics revealed skill gaps in research methods coursework. Faculty redesigned key assignments based on dashboard insights and closed the competency gap within a single semester. The department's accreditation self-study cited the data as evidence of continuous improvement.

Engineering Department

Hidden skill tracking showed first-year students were strong in computational thinking but weak in technical communication. Department chairs used engagement dashboards to adjust lab activities across all sections, resulting in measurable improvement in both written and oral presentation skills.

Nursing Program

Clinical competencies required for accreditation were automatically tracked across classroom and clinical placements. The program generated complete evidence packets with minimal faculty effort, saving an estimated 40 hours of documentation work during their accreditation cycle.

Education Program

Improved alignment between learning outcomes and student evidence across practicum placements and coursework. Faculty used analytics to ensure candidates met InTASC standards consistently, resulting in higher edTPA pass rates.
